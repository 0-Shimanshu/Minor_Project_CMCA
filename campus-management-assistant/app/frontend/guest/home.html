Why This Platform Exists

Campus-related information is often scattered across notice boards, messaging groups, and informal communication channels. This fragmentation leads to confusion, misinformation, and missed updates.

The Campus Management Assistant addresses this issue by acting as a single verified source of truth, where all information is published or reviewed by authorized campus personnel before reaching users.

Public notices are official campus announcements that are accessible to all users without requiring login. These notices typically include general academic updates, campus-wide events, administrative deadlines, and information intended for broad visibility.

Every public notice displayed on this platform is either published directly by campus administrators or approved by authorized moderators, ensuring accuracy, authenticity, and reliability.

ü§ñ Public AI Chatbot

The public AI chatbot allows guests to ask general questions related to campus facilities, procedures, and publicly available information. It is designed to assist users without requiring authentication.

The chatbot responds strictly using verified campus documents stored within the system. If sufficient information is not available, the chatbot explicitly states that the answer cannot be provided, preventing misinformation or assumptions.
